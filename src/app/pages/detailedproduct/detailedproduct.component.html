Steel Wardrobe | Cupboard | Almirah | Bero

Description
A.S.K 3 Star Steels Cupboards are made from high quality steel either from TATA or JSW. Our Luxury Wardrobes are custom designed with capacious features to maintain a well-organised and tidy arrangement. They are manufactured through world class technologies ensuring high tenacity and perfect structure.

The cupboards are available in 6 vibrant colours - 4 Solid (Pink, Blue, Green, Purple) and 3 Wooden patterns (Rosewood, Walnut, Teak).

Steel Bureau (Bero) Dimensions: Swarna 2 Door 36" Cupboard, Akshaya 2 Door 42" Almirah, Vajra 3 Door 54" Wardrobe.

Features: Weight - 72 Kg to 118 Kg (All our cupboards are heavy weight); Full size Dressing mirror (Optional); Pipe structure ensuring a Strong and Sturdy build; Powder coating which is tougher and long-lasting; European Style Soft Close Hydraulic Steel Hinge; European standard Lockers for safe keeping valuables; Bottom Suitcase locker.
